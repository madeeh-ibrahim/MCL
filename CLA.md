# MCL Contributor Agreement — Assignment (v2.0, interim)

Thank you for your interest in contributing to MCL (Madeeh Chaotic Lock), a
project owned by **Madeeh Ibrahim** ("the Project Owner"). To keep ownership of
the project unambiguous, the Project Owner must have on file this signed
Agreement from each Contributor **before** any contribution is merged.

> **Interim policy.** This is a deliberately conservative, full-assignment
> agreement adopted while the Project Owner obtains specialist legal advice on
> contribution arrangements. It may be revised after that review. Until then,
> contributions are accepted only under this assignment model. (External
> contributions may also be paused entirely — see `CONTRIBUTING.md`.)

This Agreement is adapted from copyright-assignment CLAs of the type used by the
Free Software Foundation and the Apache Software Foundation Corporate/Individual
CLAs, on a full-transfer basis.

## 1. Definitions

- **"You"** means the individual who submits a Contribution and signs this
  Agreement.
- **"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship, including any
  modifications or additions to existing work, that You intentionally submit to
  the Project in any form (for example, a pull request, patch, or attachment).

**Precedence over platform default terms.** This Agreement is the "separate
agreement" contemplated by hosting-platform terms of service (including GitHub
Terms of Service, "License Grant to Other Users"), and **supersedes** any
default rule that would otherwise license a Contribution merely under the
repository's posted license. Where this Agreement has been accepted by You, the
assignment in Section 2 governs Your Contribution, notwithstanding any
platform-default "same terms as the repository license" provision. No
Contribution will be merged into the Project until this Agreement has been
accepted by the contributor (enforced via the CLA-assistant check), so that the
assignment — not the platform default — applies to every merged Contribution.

## 2. Assignment of Copyright (Full Transfer)

> **Current project policy (interim, pending expert review).** To keep ownership
> of the project unambiguous while the project owner obtains specialist legal
> advice on contribution arrangements, contributions are accepted **only on a
> full-assignment basis** at this time. This is a deliberately conservative
> position and may be revised (e.g., to a license-back model) after that review.

You hereby **assign and transfer to the Project Owner (Madeeh Ibrahim) all
right, title, and interest worldwide**, including all copyright and related
rights, in and to Your Contribution, to the fullest extent permitted by
applicable law. The Project Owner becomes the sole owner and author-in-fact of
the Contribution for all purposes of exploitation, licensing, and
commercialization, and may deal with it without any further consent from,
accounting to, or obligation toward You.

**License fallback.** To the extent any right cannot be assigned by law
(including any inalienable right, or any jurisdiction that does not permit such
assignment), You instead grant the Project Owner a perpetual, worldwide,
exclusive, irrevocable, royalty-free, fully sublicensable and transferable
license to exercise that right, and You waive, and agree not to assert, any such
right against the Project Owner or its licensees and successors to the maximum
extent permitted by law.

**No joint authorship or ownership.** Submitting a Contribution does **not**
create joint authorship of, or joint ownership in, the Contribution or the
Project as a whole, and gives You **no** consent rights over the Project Owner's
licensing, commercial use, sale, transfer, or other exploitation of the Project.
The Project as a whole is owned and controlled solely by the Project Owner.

**Moral rights.** Where applicable law recognizes inalienable moral rights, You
retain them and the Project Owner will provide reasonable attribution; You agree
not to exercise moral rights in a way that would prevent the Project Owner from
exercising the rights assigned or licensed above.

## 4. Project Owner's Rights (consequence of assignment)

As a result of the assignment in Section 2, the Project Owner owns Your
Contribution outright and may use, license, relicense, and sublicense it under
**any license** — including proprietary, commercial, source-available, or
multiple licenses simultaneously (dual-licensing) — and may sell, transfer, or
assign the Project (including Your Contribution) in whole or in part, all
without any further consent from, accounting to, or obligation toward You. This
enables the Project Owner to offer paid commercial licenses and to transfer the
Project cleanly. These rights are irrevocable.
