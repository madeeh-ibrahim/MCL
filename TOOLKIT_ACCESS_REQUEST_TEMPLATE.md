with the subject line: `MCL Adversarial Toolkit Access Request`.

See `TOOLKIT_ACCESS_POLICY.md` for the full policy.

   [ ] I accept the Security Research & Evaluation Grant, including the

---

Before the toolkit is delivered, you will be asked to sign `TOOLKIT_ACCESS_AGREEMENT.md` (electronic signature is fine) — this is stronger than the checkboxes above and protects both sides.

*Submitting a request does not guarantee access. The maintainer aims to respond
within 7 calendar days. Access may be declined or revoked per the policy; a
decline reflects the distribution policy, not a judgment about you.*
